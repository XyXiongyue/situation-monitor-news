Yang Ming Receives AFLAS Award for Best Sustainable Shipping Line

2026-06-29 05:02

inInternational Shipping News29/06/2026

The Asian Freight, Logistics and Supply Chain Awards (AFLAS), organized by the renowned shipping publication Asia Cargo News, is one of the most prestigious honors in the global logistics and supply chain industry. In 2026, Yang Ming Marine Transport Corporation (“Yang Ming”) was once again recognized at the AFLAS Awards, marking its eleventh consecutive year of recognition. Mr. Hogan Huang, Assistant Vice President of Huan Ming (Shanghai) International Shipping Agency Co., Ltd. attended the award ceremony held in Shanghai on behalf of Yang Ming. The Company extends its sincere appreciation to customers for their continued trust and support.

Since 2016, Yang Ming has been recognized at the AFLAS Awards for eleven consecutive years. This year, Yang Ming received the Best Sustainable Shipping Line award for the first time, recognizing the Company’s long-term commitment to low-carbon transformation and sustainable shipping. In recent years, Yang Ming has continued to modernize its fleet, with its 15,500 TEU LNG dual-fuel containerships entering service in 2026. Together with smart vessel management, energy-efficient operations and various carbon reduction initiatives, these efforts continue to improve energy efficiency and reduce carbon emissions, demonstrating the Company’s commitment to achieving net-zero emissions.

Amid global supply chain restructuring and rapidly evolving market conditions, Yang Ming continues to strengthen its fleet capacity and expand its global service network while accelerating fleet modernization and low-carbon transformation. Through network expansion, fleet renewal and the adoption of cleaner energy solutions, Yang Ming continues to enhance operational efficiency and service quality, providing customers worldwide with convenient, low-carbon and safe transportation services while reinforcing its resilience and long-term competitiveness.Source: Yang Ming
